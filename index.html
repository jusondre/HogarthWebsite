The Chronicles of Hogarth

A world ancient beyond memory

Hogarth Academy of Witchcraft & Wizardry is set within the Magickal World of 1955 — a realm built by Cxyb3ria and Jusondre, born from years of shared imagination. What began as a Discord roleplay is now growing into a full Minecraft experience, carrying with it a world of original characters, history, lore, and spells crafted entirely by its founders.

Four great houses have vied for honour within these walls for generations. The sorting has never been wrong. Allegiances forged here last lifetimes.

Whether you seek knowledge in the restricted section, glory on the pitch, or power in the dungeons — the castle is watching, and it remembers.

"Courage is not the absence of fear — it is what you carry into the darkness alongside it."
— From the Founding Charter of Hogarth

The castle itself predates the written record of magical Britain. Its towers have borne witness to duels, discoveries, and disasters alike.

Hogarth's walls are not merely stone — they are memory. Every corridor holds an echo. Every portrait holds a judgment. To walk these halls is to become part of something older than any house, older than any rivalry.

The world of 1955 is a time of rebuilding and tension. Old wounds from earlier conflicts have not fully healed. New threats gather in the margins. And within the castle, a new generation of students prepares — knowingly or not — for whatever is coming.
