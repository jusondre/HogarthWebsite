The Chronicles of Hogarth

A world ancient
beyond memory

Hogarth Academy of Witchcraft & Wizardry is set within the Magickal World of 1955 — a realm built by Cxyb3ria and Ondre, born from years of shared imagination. What began as a Discord roleplay is now growing into a full Minecraft experience, carrying with it a world of original characters, history, lore, and spells crafted entirely by its founders.

The castle itself predates the written record of magical Britain. Its towers have borne witness to duels, discoveries, and disasters alike. Hogarth's walls are not merely stone — they are memory. Every corridor holds an echo. Every portrait holds a judgment. To walk these halls is to become part of something older than any house, older than any rivalry.

The World of 1955

The year is 1955. A decade has passed since the end of the Great War, but peace in the wizarding world remains fragile — a thin parchment stretched over a wound that never fully closed. In the shadows of post-war Britain, dark magic is rising again. Covens long thought scattered have reformed. Ancient curses, sealed away in the restricted archives of the Ministry, have begun reappearing in places they have no right to be. The Trio of Maleficium — the three Unforgivable Curses — are being practiced openly in certain circles, whispered about in others.

The victims are not only wizarding folk. Muggles are dying. Bodies found drained of life with no Muggle explanation — no wound, no illness, no reason the non-magical world can name. The Ministry of Magic is stretched thin, its Aurors running ragged, its public statements growing increasingly unconvincing. Something moves in the margins of the world, and it is not subtle.

But the danger does not flow in only one direction. The Muggle world, scarred by its own war and gripped by post-war suspicion, has begun to hunt back. Reports from across the continent speak of organised Muggle groups — men who have seen too much, who cannot explain what they have witnessed and have chosen fear over reason. Wizarding families have gone missing. Communities have had to relocate overnight. The Statute of Secrecy grows more difficult to uphold with each passing month. Some within the Ministry argue that it was never designed for a world this fractured.

Into this atmosphere of tension and buried dread, a new generation of students arrives at Hogarth. Most come knowing nothing of what stirs beyond the castle walls. The professors teach their lessons. The houses compete for the cup. Life, on the surface, continues as it always has. But the castle remembers every war that has come before — and it is watching.

The Four Houses

Four great houses have vied for honour within these walls for generations. The sorting has never been wrong. Allegiances forged here last lifetimes.

🦅
Ravenclaw
Wit & Wisdom
Those who seek knowledge above all else find their home beneath the eagle's wing. Ravenclaws ask questions that make professors uncomfortable — and answer them anyway.
🦁
Gryffindor
Bravery & Nerve
The lion's house has always attracted those who act before they think — and somehow come out the other side. In 1955, Gryffindor's halls are quieter than usual. Several alumni have not returned from Ministry duties.
🐍
Slytherin
Ambition & Cunning
Slytherin has always been misread. The serpent's house produces not villains, but survivors — those who understand that the world requires strategy as much as courage. In troubled times, Slytherins endure.
🦡
Hufflepuff
Loyalty & Patience
Underestimated. Always underestimated. Hufflepuffs do not seek glory — but when the castle has needed protecting most, it has been the badger's students who stood at the door and did not move.
