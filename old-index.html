The Chronicles of Hogarth

A world ancient beyond memory

Long before the first students crossed the threshold, the grounds of Hogarth were a place of deep and unresolved magic. Ancient ley lines converge beneath the courtyard flagstones; the portraits are restless; the staircases still change on their own schedule.

Four great houses have vied for honour within these walls for generations. The sorting has never been wrong. Allegiances forged here last lifetimes.

Whether you seek knowledge in the restricted section, glory on the pitch, or power in the dungeons — the castle is watching, and it remembers.

"Courage is not the absence of fear — it is what you carry into the darkness alongside it."
— From the Founding Charter of Hogarth

The Arcane Lexicon

Spells of Hogarth

Combat & Duelling
ExpelliarmusDisarms an opponent, sending their wand flying from their grasp.
StupefyStuns the target, leaving them temporarily immobile and senseless.
ProtegoRaises a magical shield that deflects spells and minor physical forces.
IncarcerousConjures ropes that bind the target — useful in capture or restraint.
ConfringoCauses the target to explode in a burst of searing heat and force.
DiffindoSlashes or severs the target — cloth, rope, or enchanted bindings alike.
Utility & Exploration
LumosIgnites a bright light at the tip of the wand, illuminating dark corridors.
AccioSummons an object to the caster's hand from a distance.
AlohomoraUnlocks mundane locks and certain enchanted ones.
RevelioReveals hidden objects, concealed passages, and magical disguises nearby.
Wingardium LeviosaLevitates objects with fine control over movement through the air.
ReparoMends broken objects, restoring them to their original whole form.
Transfiguration & Charms
EvanescoVanishes an object entirely, dissolving it from existence.
EngorgioEnlarges the target, increasing its size and mass dramatically.
ReducioShrinks an enlarged object or creature back to a manageable scale.
ConfundoBewilders the target, leaving them confused and easily misled.
SilencioRenders the target completely mute — neither voice nor wand-sound escapes.
Dark Arts & Restricted

CrucioOne of the three Unforgivable Curses — inflicts unbearable torment upon the victim.Forbidden

The Academic Curriculum

Subjects taught at Hogarth

🧪
Potions
The art and science of brewing. Students learn to combine magical ingredients with precision — a wrongly measured ingredient can be as dangerous as any curse.
Years I–VII
⭐
Transfiguration
Among the most complex branches of magic. Students learn to alter the form, nature, and very essence of objects — and eventually living creatures.
Years I–VII
🌿
Herbology
The study of magical plants, fungi, and their properties. Many healing remedies and potion ingredients begin in the greenhouses of Hogarth.
Years I–VII
🔮
Divination
The imprecise art of reading the future through tea leaves, crystal balls, and dream interpretation. Widely debated — but occasionally, unnervingly accurate.
Years III–VII
🌑
Defence Against the Dark Arts
Practical training in countercurses, protective spells, and the identification of dangerous creatures and dark enchantments. A matter of survival.
Years I–VII
🦎
Care of Magical Creatures
Students encounter, handle, and study the beasts of the magical world — from docile Nifflers to creatures whose temperaments demand great respect.
Years III–VII
✨
Charms
The foundations of practical magic. Students master wand technique, incantation, and the precise application of magical will.
Years I–VII
🌙
Astronomy
Observation of the night sky and its influence upon magical practice. Conducted from the castle's highest tower, often in the cold hours before dawn.
Years I–V
📜
History of Magic
A record of the magical world's great conflicts, founding figures, and pivotal events. Dry in delivery — but those who study it find its secrets run deep.
