How to Begin Your Journey

Five steps stand between you and the castle gates.

I
Join the Discord
Every journey begins here. Join our community, read the rules, and introduce yourself to the castle.
Join Discord
II
Create Your Character
Craft your original witch or wizard. Name, backstory, house preference — this is who you will be within these walls.
Creating a Character
III
Get Approved
Submit your character for review. The staff will read your application and grant entry to those who are ready.
Meet the Staff
IV
Receive Your Letter
Once approved, your Hogarth acceptance letter arrives in Discord. Your house is revealed. The castle is expecting you.
V
Begin Your Journey
Open Minecraft Java 1.20+, connect to mc.hogarth.net and step through the gates. Your story starts now.
