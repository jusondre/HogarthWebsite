How to Begin Your Journey

Five steps stand between you and the castle gates.

Hogarth School of Witchcraft
I
First Step
Join the Discord
Every journey begins here. Join our community, read the rules, and introduce yourself to the castle. The gates are open to all who seek entry.
Join the Community →
II
Second Step
Create Your Character
Craft your original witch or wizard. Choose a name, weave a backstory, and declare your house preference — this is who you will be within these ancient walls.
Creating a Character →
III
Third Step
Get Approved
Submit your character for review. Our staff will read your application with care, and grant entry to those who are ready to step through the threshold.
Meet the Staff →
IV
Fourth Step
Receive Your Letter
Once approved, your Hogarth acceptance letter arrives in Discord. Your house is revealed at last. The castle has been expecting you all along.
V
Final Step
Begin Your Journey
Open Minecraft Java 1.20+, connect to mc.hogarth.net, and step through the gates. Your story — and everything that follows — starts now.
