About Hogarth

Hogarth Academy of Witchcraft & Wizardry is a roleplay community set within an original fictional universe, based on Discord and soon to be Minecraft, created by Cxyb3ria and Jusondre.

Wizarding World

Hogarth Academy is heavily inspired by J. K. Rowling's Wizarding World. Anything mentioned directly about Rowling's Wizarding World within this document — especially by our members — is © & ™ Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc., with Publishing Rights © J. K. Rowling.

Artwork & Graphics

All graphics and artwork produced by Cxyb3ria for Hogarth Academy can only be shared outside the community by Cxyb3ria and Jusondre, unless stated otherwise. If you desire similar graphics and artworks for your personal project(s), please contact Cxyb3ria on Discord or Instagram.

Sidenote

Some terminology used within Hogarth Academy is derived from Latin translations of words and phrases. Subjects such as Magickal Creatures are derived from Scottish and Irish folklore, which belong to the public domain.
